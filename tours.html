  2. Day 2: Full day observing elephants in Amboseli.
  3. Day 3: Transfer to Tsavo West, visit Mzima Springs.
  4. Day 4: Full day game drive exploring Tsavo's volcanic landscapes.
  5. Day 5: Early morning drive, journey back to Nairobi.
