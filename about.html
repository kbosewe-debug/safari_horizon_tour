About Our Company

For those who demand unparalleled excellence, SAFARI HORIZON TOURS delivers the pinnacle of luxury travel in East Africa. We blend high-end comfort with raw, untamed adventure, offering bespoke safaris and exclusive coastal retreats that cater to the discerning traveler. Our commitment to flawless execution and deeply immersive experiences has earned us the prestigious "Kenya's Leading Safari Tour Operator" title at the World Travel Awards. Beyond the accolades, our true reward is the trust of hundreds of families, honeymooners, and solo adventurers who choose us year after year for our legendary 24/7 on-ground care and private, elite guiding. Fun Fact: Our customized safari cruisers are equipped with onboard espresso machines and specialized camera mounts, ensuring you never miss a shot—or your morning brew—while tracking the Great Migration. Your dream itinerary is waiting; let our award-winning team bring it to life.
